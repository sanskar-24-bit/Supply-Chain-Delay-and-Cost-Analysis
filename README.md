# Samsung Supply Chain and Logistics Dashboard

## Overview
An end-to-end Power BI dashboard built to monitor and analyze Samsung's supply 
chain performance for the Galaxy S25 Ultra across 6 key modules.

## Key Features

1. *Home Page* – Clean landing page with navigation to all dashboard modules: 
   Overview, Supplier, Manufacturer, Shipment, and Customer.

2. *Overview Page* – High-level KPIs including ₹186.86M gross revenue, 
   176.95M total revenue, 48.56M profit, and quick-access summaries of supplier 
   lead times, inventory stock, shipment delays, and platform-wise revenue.

3. *Supplier Analysis* – Tracks 7 supplier IDs across countries (China, Japan, 
   South Korea, Taiwan, India, Vietnam) with metrics on avg lead time (11.53 days), 
   order quantity (129K), unit cost trends by month, and quality score (96.63).

4. *Inventory & Production* – Monitors 4M units produced, 24K defective units, 
   defect rate of 0.6%, inventory value (160K), safety stock (89K), and current 
   stock vs reorder points across 15+ product SKUs.

5. *Shipment Tracking* – Analyzes 8K total shipments across 9 carriers (C.H. 
   Robinson, XPO Logistics, FedEx, etc.), with 573 total delays, 75.29% delivery 
   rate, and delay breakdown by reason (carrier capacity, documentation, customs, 
   weather, etc.).

6. *Supply & Revenue* – Visualizes monthly revenue and profit trends, platform 
   performance (Amazon, Flipkart, Best Buy, MediaMarkt, Samsung Direct), discount 
   % by product, and revenue split by channel type (Online/Retailer/Direct).

7. *Tools & Technologies* – Built using *Power BI Desktop* with DAX measures 
   for KPI calculations, custom visuals for donut/bar/line charts, and structured 
   data modeling across supplier, inventory, shipment, and sales tables.
